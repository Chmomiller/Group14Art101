N-95 Masks

The Benefits of wearing N-95 Masks

It slows the spread of the virus.
It help people who may have the virus and do not know it from transmitting it to others.
The low cost of the mask can be used as an additional, voluntary public health measure.

Downfalls of wearing a N-95 Mask

These supplies are in high demand and there is not enough for everyone. Because of their efficiency in blocking virus they should be saved for health workers that are in high contact with COVID
Touching the mask while wearing it can allow the germs to spread to your face.
PSA: Having a cloth mask, does not mean you do not need to social distance!

How to find an N-95 Mask

These masks are available at most department stores.
Though the safest way to purchase these masks is online!
