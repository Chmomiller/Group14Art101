N95 Masks

Pros

Fitted to create a seal around your face. Blocks 95% of virus from coming in contact with the mouth and nose.

Cons

These supplies are in high demand and there is not enough for everyone. Because of their efficiency in blocking virus they should be saved for health workers that are in high contact with COVID

How to get N95 Masks

These masks are availbe and most department stores, though the safest way to buy them is to order them online. You can purchase these masks on both amazon and ebay.
