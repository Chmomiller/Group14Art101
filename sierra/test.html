Cloth Masks

The Benefits of Wearing Cloth Masks
It slows the spread of the virus. It help people who may have the virus and do not know it from transmitting it to others. The low cost of the mask can be used as an additional, voluntary public health measure.

Downfalls of wearing a Cloth Mask
PSA: Having a cloth mask, does not mean you do not need to social distance! Touching the mask while wearing it can allow the germs to spread to your face. After wearing consider your mask contaminated, you need to machine wash frequently.
